HOSPITALS

Santa Teresita Municipal Hospital: This is the main public healthcare center in the Partido de La Costa. It provides 24/7 emergency care, a clinical lab, X-ray services, inpatient care, and specialties such as pediatrics, gynecology, and dentistry. Located at Av. 41 and 16, it offers free medical attention to the entire community.

Old Santa Teresita Hospital: This building served for decades as the district’s main hospital. Although it no longer functions as a healthcare facility, it holds historical significance for the local community. Some municipal services are currently operating from this location.

CONSUMED Medical Center (Consultorios Médicos Santa Teresita): A private health center offering specialized medical services, diagnostic imaging, laboratory testing, and other medical procedures. It is a key option for residents seeking private healthcare in the area.
