SCHOOLS

Technical School No. 1 – Santa Teresita: Founded over 54 years ago, this is the largest school in the district with more than 1,300 students. It offers specializations in Programming, Tourism, and Architectural Drafting. It is currently building its own new facilities at 104th and 124th streets.

Secondary School No. 12 “Hero of Malvinas José Luis Rodríguez”: This school has a focus on Social Sciences. It is located on 124th Street between 104 and 105 in Santa Teresita. It serves approximately 549 students in a single-shift schedule and promotes values such as freedom, equality, and solidarity.

Secondary School No. 38: Currently, there is no confirmed public information about this school in the Partido de La Costa. It is recommended to verify whether it refers to another location or operates under a different name.

Colegio Modelo Santa Teresita: Located at 48 San Luis Street, this private school promotes a creative and personalized education model. Its motto is: “Each Modernista discovers, explores, and grows.” It offers education from kindergarten to secondary school, emphasizing values and holistic development.
